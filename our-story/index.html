BASIS STORY

Life on your Terms



Basis is a unified experience, where you can learn about personal finance, consume financial content and invest through a first of its kind mobile app.


Basis was started in 2018 with a simple, yet grand vision: that every retail investor is empowered and informed to make the personal finance decisions that are right for her. We are building an inclusive investing platform that is crafted for the emerging investor, the Indian woman.


Basis is a SEBI Registered Investment Adviser. This means that (a) we are regulated by India's securities regulator and (b) your financial interests are paramount to us. As an adviser, we offer direct plans on mutual fund schemes, and provide objective and unbiased advice. There are no hidden commissions involved.


For your information, most companies who sell mutual funds today are distributors, and they receive commissions from mutual fund houses for the funds they suggest to customers. This could result in advice that could be misaligned with customers' interests. Investing in mutual fund plans through distributors (versus an adviser like us) also ends up being more expensive for the customer.


As a passionate, mission-driven team we want to make a positive difference to how Indian women grow and manage their money.
